Rishikesh Chess Club

Hello friends your most welcome in Rishikesh chess club website. Mr. Sher Singh Thapa is the founder of chess club and the this was registered in 2014. Club has organized many tournaments in Rishikesh and near other areas as well. The main purpose was to build this group to provide a platform for Rishikesh chess players so that they can improve their chess skills. Now there are many growing chess players who are enjoying the the game and participating at national level.

Chess Club Members

Sher Singh Thapa

Mr. Sher Singh Thapa (Founder and President of Rishikesh Chess club):- Main credit goes to this single person whose creative mind thought to provide better environment for local chess player who were eagerly looking for this type of platform. After long time effort and several meetings Mr. Thapa created a team of few chess players and later formed this club. His dedication and passion towards chess and other games are really appreciable. He is an international Tug-of-war player and gold medalist as well.
